  1. Something I Like: physical game and sunset photography.

    • Football features key lines on the field such as the goal line, end line, sidelines, end zone, and yard lines, which structure gameplay. The line of scrimmage and kickoff line are crucial for starting plays, while hash marks help position the ball, and goalposts are used for scoring. Playing football provides numerous benefits, including improved physical fitness, enhanced coordination, and greater teamwork. It promotes discipline, builds mental toughness, and encourages strategic thinking. The sport also fosters social interaction, develops leadership abilities, and provides stress relief. Additionally, football strengthens community bonds and boosts confidence through teamwork and achievement.

    • The sunset is a daily marvel, a transition between day and night that fills the sky with vibrant hues of orange, pink, purple, and gold. Sunset photography captures the stunning interplay of light and color as the sun dips below the horizon. The warm, golden hour light creates dramatic shadows and highlights, making subjects appear more vibrant and landscapes more striking. Photographers often focus on the sky, framing the horizon, clouds, and any silhouettes, like trees or buildings, to add depth to their images. Timing is key, as the colors change quickly, from bright oranges and pinks to deeper purples and blues.

His work has been displayed across three continents from the largest video screen on the planet in Times Square. From an early age Reiffer developed his interest in photography through spending time with his father, watching him develop images in a self-built darkroom in their family home. Notably angered in 2015 by the increasing levels of post-processing that was becoming acceptable in the photographic world.


  3. Someplace I Cherish: The Ocean

    • The ocean is a place I deeply cherish for its vastness, beauty, and calming presence. Its rhythmic waves and endless horizon offer a sense of peace and tranquility, making it an ideal spot for reflection and relaxation. The ocean's ever-changing colors and moods—from calm blues to stormy grays—mirror the ebb and flow of life. It's a source of inspiration, with its boundless energy and serene depths. Whether standing on the shore or listening to the crashing waves, the ocean provides a moment to pause, recharge, and connect with nature. It reminds me of the world's natural wonders and the simple beauty found in stillness.
